The game has 16 damage types that can be used.

Damage types do more or less damage to enemies and have specific effects.

Impact: Deals 1.5x damage to Grineer, Kuva Grineer, Scaldra, and Anarchs

Puncture: Deals 1.5x damage to Corpus, Orokin, and The Corrupted

Slash: Deals 1.5x damage to the Infested and Narmer

Cold: Deals 1.5x damage to Sentients, 0.5x damage to Techrot

Electricity: Deals 1.5x damage to Corpus Amalgam, The Murmur, and Anarchs. Also deals 1.5x damage to shields.

Heat: Deals 1.5x damage to Infested, 0.5x damage to Kuva Grineer

Toxin: Deals 1.5x damage to Narmer, bypasses shields and directly attacks health. Does not bypass overguard

Blast: Deals 1.5x damage to Infested Deimos, 0.5x damage to Corpus Amalgam

Corrosive: Deals 1.5x damage to Grineer, Kuva Grineer, and Scaldra, 0.5x damage to Sentients

Gas: Deals 1.5x damage to Infested Deimos and Techrot, 0.5x damage to Scaldra

Magnetic: Deals 1.5x damage to Corpus, Corpus Amalgam, and Techrot, 0.5x damage to Narmer. Also deals 1.5x damage to shields and overguard

Radiation: Deals 1.5x damage to Sentients and the Murmur, 0.5x damage to Orokin and Corrupted

Viral: Deals 1.5x damage to Orokin, 0.5x damage to Infested Deimos and the Murmur.

Void: Deals 1.5x damage to the Zariman, 0.5x damage to Tenno and Stalker. Also deals 1.5x damage to overguard

Tau: Deals 1.5x damage to Tenno

True: Deals equal damage to all enemies

//

Operators do not take damage based on damage type.

All damage taken by operators is counted as true, without increase or decrease in taken damage.
