## Physical Damage Types

### **Impact**
- **Increased Damage To:** Grineer, Kuva Grineer, Scaldra

### **Puncture**
- **Increased Damage To:** Corpus, Orokin

### **Slash**
- **Increased Damage To:** Infested, Narmer
- **Special:** **Bypasses Armor**
---

## Elemental Damage Types

### **Cold**
- **Increased Damage To:** Sentient
- **Decreased Damage To:** Techrot

### **Electricity**
- **Increased Damage To:** Corpus Amalgam, The Murmur
- **Special:** **+50% damage to Shields**
### **Heat**
- **Increased Damage To:** Infested
- **Decreased Damage To:** Kuva Grineer
### **Toxin**
- **Increased Damage To:** Narmer
- **Special:** **Bypasses Shields**, **Does *not* bypass Overguard**
### **Blast**
- **Increased Damage To:** Infested Deimos
- **Decreased Damage To:** Corpus Amalgam
### **Corrosive**
- **Increased Damage To:** Grineer, Kuva Grineer, Scaldra
- **Decreased Damage To:** Sentient
### **Magnetic**
- **Increased Damage To:** Corpus, Corpus Amalgam, Techrot
- **Decreased Damage To:** Narmer
- **Special:** **+50% damage to Shields and Overguard**
### **Radiation**
- **Increased Damage To:** Sentient, The Murmur
- **Decreased Damage To:** Orokin
### **Viral**
- **Increased Damage To:** Orokin
- **Decreased Damage To:** Infested Deimos, The Murmur
### **Void**
- **Increased Damage To:** Zariman beings, Void Manifestations
- **Special:**
  - **+50% damage to Overguard and Shields**
  - **Removes all Sentient & Caliban adaptations** *(even without applying Void procs)*
### **Tau**
- **Neutral Damage:** No bonuses or penalties

### **True**
- **Neutral Damage:** No bonuses or penalties
- **Special:**
  - **Bypasses Shields (but NOT Overguard)**
  - **Ignores Armor**
