Operators refers to the player.

The Operator has a certain number of health and shields.

This can be damaged by Win Conditions. However they can be distracted by enemy tanks taunting.
However, Win Conditions with an OnPlay ability cannot be distracted by tanks.

Attacker cards can also damage this health if there are no enemy cards present.

Operators take increased damage from Void on health, and Magnetic and Void on shields.
Toxin damage can bypass Operator shields.

All Win Condition cards without a specified damage type to attack the operator with deal True damage.
