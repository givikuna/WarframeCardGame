Focus Schools are chosen by the player as a part of their deck.

Each focus school gives the player an ability they can use.

There are five focus schools: Zenurik, Vazarin, Madurai, Naramon, Unairu.

Each of these 5 have an ability associated with them that costs credits to use:


Zenurik:
Wellspring (500 credits): give as many credits as the card with the highest max health, in exchange the card with the highest max health dies.

Vazarin:
Mending Soul (500 credits): instantly restores 100 health and gives 50 overguard to all friendly cards.

Madurai:
Phoenix Talons (600 credits): for the next 2 turns +30% damage.

Naramon:
Strategic Strike (1000 credits): all OnTurn cards'll repeat twice for this turn.

Unairu:
Caustic Strike (1000 credits): instantly strips all shields from enemies
