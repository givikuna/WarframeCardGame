Cephalons are chosen by the player to be a part of his or her deck.

They are not cards.

Selecting a Cephalon gives a modifier that will be applied for the entire game:

The following are the cephalons and the effects associated with them:

Apnar (Implemented):
When a card is destroyed it deals 35 damage to the enemy Operator.

Cordylon:
You're granted 3 random cards in your hand at the beginning of the game

Cy:
Your first card played each turn has its cost reduced by one rank.

Jordas:
Whenever a card dies on your side, it has a 30% chance to summon a free Charger to your hand.

Melica:
Every 3 turns a random card on your board gains +50 max health and +50 max shields.
This also heals the card by 10 health and grants it 10 shields.

Ordis:
Every time you play a card, 10% chance to have its cost refunded to you.

Samodeus:
At the start of the game, one random attacker card in your deck is going to gain the following OnTurn action:
Samodeus's Blessing: Deal 50 magnetic damage, 30% status chance, 10% critical chance, 1.5x multiplier, per turn to the card with the highest effective HP.
The said card will also go up in rarity by one level.

Sark:
For every 500 damage dealt to the enemy in totality you gain 150 credits.
For every 200 damage dealt to the enemy operator you gain 20 credits.

Vull:
Every time any card on the board dies your operator gains +15 health.
