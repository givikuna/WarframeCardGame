Cards can be sorted into the following categories:

Tank:
a tank card has an ability that allows it to absorb away damage from other cards and the operator

Attacker:
an attacker card deals damage to other cards on the board
the targeted card is selected based on the conditions of the card

Win Condition:
win condition cards attack the operator and only the operator
their attacks can be blocked by various effects and taunting from tanks

Support:
support cards provide support to their allies without dealing damage or tanking
this can be in the form of damage boosting, healing, generating credits, applying useful status effects and so on

Summoner:
summoner cards are cards that summon other cards for various purposes
these cards'll place down a card that might tank, be a win condition, a support, or attacker.
they add a body to the board and allow the player to use odd tactics in the game
