Gameplay will be done as such.

Both players will be playing cards at the same time, given a 25 second window to figure out what cards to play / whether to use focus school ability or not.
After both players have played, if it is the first turn, the cards that'll go first will be chosen randomly to be the first player's (whichever one connected earliest to the queue). From that turn onwards, the player whose cards'll go first will be alternating.

This'll happen in this order:
First Player's Focus School Ability
Second Player's Focus School Ability
First Player's Syndicate Effect
Second Player's Syndicate Effect
First Player's OnPlays
Second Player's OnPlays
First Player's OnTurns
Second Player's OnTurns
First Player's Cephalon Ability
Second Player's Cephalon Ability

Once a card is played it'll be placed on the board and do nothing until its turn comes even if it isn't going to have a chance to do its OnPlay ability until all the enemy cards have gone first.
An OnPlay card may die this way if it is placed when the player's cards wouldn't be the first ones to go.
Therefore, playing OnPlay cards must be done carefully.

The player who gets priority each turn'll marked in the game to avoid confusion.

Each turn players'll earn 100 credits.

On the 11th turn and beyond players'll earn 175 credits.

20th turn and beyond players'll earn 250 credits.

30th turn and beyond players'll earn 400 credits.

40th turn and beyond players'll earn 1000 credits per turn.
