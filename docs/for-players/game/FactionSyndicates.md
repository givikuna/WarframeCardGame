A Faction Syndicate is one of the following 6:

Arbiters of Hexis
Steel Meridian
The Perrin Sequence
New Loka
Red Veil
Cephalon Suda

A Faction Syndicate is chosen by the player in their deck.

This will affect the Syndicate Effect.
An effect that'll go off every 1000 damage dealt by the player.

Steel Meridian: gives the effect of Justice, which deals 50 Blast Damage and status to all enemy cards and operator and restores 25% of max health to all cards.
Arbiters of Hexis: gives the effect of Truth, which deals 50 Gas Damage and status to all enemy cards and operator and restores 25% of max health to all cards.
Cephalon Suda: gives the effect of Entropy, which deals 50 Magnetic Damage and status to all enemy cards and operator and restores 200 health to the operator.
The Perrin Sequence: gives the effect of Sequence: which deals 50 Radiation Damage and status to all enemy cards and operator and restores 25% of max shields to all cards.
Red Veil: gives the effect of Blight, which deals 50 Viral Damage and status to all enemy cards and operator and restores 200 health to operator.
New Loka: gives the effect of Purity, which deals 50 Corrosive Damage and status to all enemy cards and operator and restores 25% of max shields to all cards.
