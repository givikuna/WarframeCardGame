Health Layers in this game are as follows:

Health, Shield and Overguard

First, damage is taken by Overguard

All possible damage is taken by Overguard and it cannot be bypassed

Shield takes the damage next.

Shields can be bypassed by Toxin damage.

Overguard takes extra damage when damaged by Magnetic or Void damage types

Shields take extra damage when damaged by Magnetic or Electricity damage types
